• Use ⬅️ ️⬆️ ➡️ ⬇️ to move the runner in the terrain.
  • Visit all of the points on the course in order from 1 to 4
  • Colors on the board represent various terrain types. Some terrain is easier and some harder to run across
    • Easy - tall "white" forrest and meadows
    • Medium - mixed forrest
    • Hard - thick forrest and water
    • Obstacle - running across black obstacles is impossible
  • Going through each of these terrains will cost you some energy. Total cost will be visible under the board.
  • Find out the path with the lowest cost and have fun!
